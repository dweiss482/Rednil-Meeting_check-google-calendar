Check your Google Calendar for event details

Your meeting links, instructions, and timing are listed inside the calendar event. Open Google Calendar and review today’s (or upcoming) events.

If you use multiple Google accounts, confirm you’re signed into the correct one.
Open the event and read the Description for links and instructions.
Confirm the time zone and the video link/location field.
If you don’t see it, check other calendars in the sidebar or search the event name.
If Calendar won’t load, refresh or sign into Google in a new tab.
Note: Some meeting links may only appear after you accept the invite.
